From first time presenter to seasoned veteran, we all grow when we take the opportunity to share our work with others. It gives us a moment for feedback, healthy vulnerability and most importantly the satisfaction of a job well done.

At Love & Matter we hold space for all kinds of artists, craftsfolk, or just dedicated individuals who want to share with their friends and family the amazing work that keeps them up at night.
