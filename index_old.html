makers need access

For anyone who has lived in NYC for more than a few weeks you know the number one resources is SPACE. It is so limited here, so how can an artist grow their craft and work in a productive way? We need access

Access to tools, techniques, storage.. and most importantly other artists who can help refine and improve our work with feedback and love.

Our goal is to create a space that fosters cross-pollination within the arts. Not just in a theoretical way, but by putting artist from varies forms of exspression in close proximity with each other. What happens when a potter can give feedback to the works of a painter? How impactful would a scuplture's thoughts be to a woodworker creating their next detailed joinery.

By providing space, tools, and resources like classes and commnity run critiques or gallery shows, we look to offer a unique experience for emerging or established creatives in Crown Heights and Bedstuy.

need a place to create?

If you're an artist or maker in the Crown Heights or Bedstuy area and need space to cultivate your practice, share your progress with others, or want to create a home for an existing class you already teach.. please connect with us!

looking ahead

updated
January 2026

We've secured a facility in Crown Heights Brooklyn and have kicked off the design and renovation process. As well as spent most of the year doing research and development to define L&M's voice, what communities we'll be serving, our ethos, what facilities and opportunities we want to offer, and our overall vision.

Heading into Winter 2026 we've prepared The Lab to host art shows and gatherings before we go under construction. You can find below the first three events we have planned.

We'll spend the rest of the winter preparing The Flat to host club meetings and micro workshops, with a goal of opening in Spring '26. We'll continue having shows and exhibitions in The Lab and plan for a large scale art/crafts event in The Hangar once the weather is nicer!

We still have a long road ahead until the full vision of Love & Matter is realized. Mainly renovating the existing building and fitting it with all the appropriate equipment for each shop. In the short term the goal is to host events as we can in our space. Two of which will be closed during construction, but The Flat will remain open for smaller scale gatherings.
